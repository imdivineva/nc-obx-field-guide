Journey 1 · Durham to Nags Head · US-64 East

From the Piedmont to the Sea

Begin among the hills and red clay of the Piedmont. Follow the land as it flattens, darkens, floods, opens into sounds, and finally narrows into the Outer Banks — narrated stop by stop, exactly where you'd meet it from the road.

Placeholder photography

This is a virtual windshield journey, not an encyclopedia entry. At every stop, the question is the same: what can you actually see from the road right here, and what does that tell you about where you are? A closer identification photo and a real map fill in what the windshield view can't.

Scroll through all 15 stops in order, or jump ahead using the route strip pinned above.

Photography throughout this draft is stand-in stock/placeholder imagery, swapped in to test layout and pacing. Real on-location, correct-season photography — the most important tier — should replace it before this concept moves forward.
