Episode 01 · Durham to Nags Head · US-64 East

From the Piedmont to the Sea

A virtual windshield journey. Begin among the hills and red clay of the Piedmont. Follow the land as it flattens, darkens, floods, opens into sounds, and finally narrows into the Outer Banks — one mile marker at a time.

PLACEHOLDER FOOTAGE — STAND-IN STOCK IMAGERY

This is a windshield documentary, not an encyclopedia entry. At every stop, the question is the same: what can you actually see from the road right here, and what does that tell you about where you are? A closer identification frame and a real satellite map fill in what the windshield can't.
