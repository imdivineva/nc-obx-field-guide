OBX Field Guide — three drafts

Same content, same idea — a field guide to the natural environments of the Outer Banks, not a tourist guide. Three different visual directions to react to.

Draft A

Field Notebook

Warm, paper-and-ink naturalist field guide feel. Serif type, cream palette, hand-annotated touches.
Draft B

Topographic Atlas

Dark chart-room feel — nautical/survey chart aesthetic, monospace labels, grid backdrop.
Draft C

Coastal Journey

Light, photography-forward scrollytelling that drives the route north to south, zone by zone.
All three are self-contained HTML files — open any of them directly, no build step required.
