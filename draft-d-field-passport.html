Driving the route, north to south

Every sound crossing is a hard edge between zones. Check off each leg as you drive it, from the mainland down to Hatteras Island.

The OBX Field Passport is an educational reference to the natural geography of the Outer Banks, not a substitute for official park or refuge guidance — always check current tide charts before walking tidal flats, marsh creeks, or inlet shorelines.
